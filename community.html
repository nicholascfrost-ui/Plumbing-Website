Committed to Our Community

We believe great plumbing service starts with faithful stewardship, practical care, and a long-term commitment to the people and neighborhoods we call home.

Rooted in Auburn and Opelika

J. Roarke Plumbing Company was built right here in East Alabama, and our team lives in the same communities we serve. That local connection shapes how we work each day, from showing up on time to treating each job with care and professionalism.

Our mission goes beyond completing service calls. We are committed to supporting the wider community through reliable work, honest communication, and trusted partnerships with local families, businesses, and organizations.

As a Christian-owned company, we view stewardship as a practical responsibility: to serve people well, use resources wisely, and conduct business with integrity. That commitment guides how we lead our team, respond to customers, and invest back into our community.
